About WOPEX

Precision Welding & Fabrication
Built to Global Standards

WOPEX Manufacturing India Pvt. Ltd. is a Swiss-collaborated multinational company delivering high-quality welding, fabrication, engineering, and inspection solutions for global industrial clients.

Founded in July 2019, WOPEX operates a modern manufacturing facility in Pune, Maharashtra (India), supported by certified welding coordinators, qualified welders, and NDT professionals. Our headquarters is located in Geneva, Switzerland.

In addition to fabrication, WOPEX also provides:

  • Third-party quality inspection
  • Welding & painting consultancy
  • Documentation & SOP development

Our Core Capabilities

End-to-end welding, fabrication, and quality solutions executed with precision, compliance, and technical excellence.

01

Welding & Fabrication

Advanced GTAW (TIG) and GMAW (MIG/MAG) welding processes for Carbon Steel, Mild Steel, Stainless Steel, and Aluminium, performed under qualified WPS and WPQR.

02

Engineering & Industrialization

Welding drawing review, feasibility studies, design-to-cost, industrialization support, jig and fixture design, and detailed workshop documentation.

03

Quality, Inspection & Compliance

In-house visual and penetrant testing, dimensional inspection, macro examination, and coordination of NABL-approved NDT and mechanical testing.
