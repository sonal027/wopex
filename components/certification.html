EN 15085-2 CL2 Certification

WOPEX Manufacturing India Pvt. Ltd. is officially certified by TÜV Rheinland Industrie Service GmbH for the welding of railway vehicles and components, in accordance with the European standard EN 15085-2:2020 + A1:2023, Classification Level CL2. This certification confirms that WOPEX fulfills stringent European requirements for quality, safety, and traceability in railway welding activities.

Certification Highlights

  • Certified Organization: Wopex Manufacturing India Pvt. Ltd.
  • Certification Standard: EN 15085-2
  • Classification Level: CL2
  • Type of Activity: P
  • Certified by: TÜV Rheinland Industrie Service GmbH
  • Certificate Validity: 24-10-2024 to 27-10-2028
  • Manufacturing Location: Pune, Maharashtra, India

This certification reinforces WOPEX’s capability to deliver safety-critical railway structures and assemblies compliant with international railway standards.

Scope of Certification – EN 15085-2 CL2

The EN 15085-2 CL2 certification covers a comprehensive scope of welding processes, materials, dimensions, and applications, ensuring WOPEX’s readiness for complex railway fabrication requirements.

Welding Processes Covered

  • Process 135 – GMAW (MIG/MAG)
  • Process 141 – GTAW (TIG)

Materials & Dimensions

  • Carbon Steel and Stainless Steel groups as per EN ISO/TR 15608
  • Plate and pipe thickness ranges from 1.4 mm up to 30 mm

Area of Application

  • New build of railway vehicles
  • Transport containers and structural assemblies
  • Supporting frames, brackets, and tensioning straps for interior equipment

All welding activities are executed under qualified WPS / WPQR, with inspection and testing aligned to European railway compliance requirements.
